*   **Email Address**: (e.g., hello@tatva.io)
*   **Phone Number**: (e.g., +91 00000 00000)
*   **Office Address**: (If applicable)
*   **Social Media Links**: Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.

## 2. Homepage Content
*   **Hero Section**: 
    *   One high-quality background image (horizontal/landscape).
    *   Main Headline (current dummy: "Books that ask to be read slowly").
*   **Highlights (The "What we believe" section)**:
    *   Provide 3 main values or pillars of your brand.
    *   For each pillar: A title, a short description, and one high-quality image.

## 3. Books & Authors
*   **Live Content**: The Sanity Studio is ready. You need to upload your real books, authors, and cover art there.
*   **Book Text**: Provide the full text of each book (for the E-reader) and remember to use `/pagebreak` or `---` where you want new pages.
*   **PDFs**: Real PDF files for the download option.

## 4. About Page Content
*   **The Story**: Detailed text about the brand's history and philosophy.
*   **Team/Values**: Names and short bios of key people (if any).
*   **Imagery**: 2-3 atmospheric photos showing your process or office.

## 5. Journal (Blog)
*   **Articles**: At least 3 real articles/essays to fill the Journal page.

## 5. Campaigns
*   **Promotional Material**: Details for any ongoing campaigns or anthologies you want to feature on the homepage.

## 6. Footer Details
*   **Copyright Name**: (e.g., Tatva Publishing 2025)
*   **Newsletter Service**: (If you want a working newsletter, provide the API key for Mailchimp, etc.)
